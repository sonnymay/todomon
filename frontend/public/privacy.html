🐲 ToDoMon Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

ToDoMon is an offline app. Your data — tasks, your dragon, coins, streaks, and progress — is stored only on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. ToDoMon does not connect to any server to run.

Purchases

If you buy ToDoMon Pro, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details. Your purchase is remembered on your device and via your Apple ID (use “Restore Purchase” to recover it).

Your data & deletion

Because everything stays on your device, deleting the app permanently removes all of your ToDoMon data. You can also reset everything in Settings → “Restart pet”.

Children

ToDoMon does not collect data from anyone, including children. It contains no ads and no external links to inappropriate content.
