Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

Use the task form on the main screen. Add a title, optional note, and difficulty. Completing the task feeds your dragon and earns coins.

How do I feed my pet?

Complete tasks to earn coins and experience for your pet. Your pet grows and evolves as you complete more to-dos!

What is the Supporter Pack?

The app is fully playable without it. The optional one-time Supporter Pack unlocks premium cosmetics, bonus celebrations, and extra coins.

How do I delete a task?

Tap the delete control on a task. Completed tasks can also be undone if you made a mistake.

I lost my data - what do I do?

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend enabling iCloud backup for your device to protect your data.

How do I report a bug?

Please open an issue on our GitHub Issues page or email us at sonnymay@hotmail.com with a description of the problem and your device and iOS version.

Privacy

ToDoMon does not collect or share your task data. Your tasks and dragon progress are stored locally on your device. Purchases are handled by Apple and RevenueCat.
