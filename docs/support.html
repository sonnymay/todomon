Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

Tap the + button on the main screen to create a new to-do item. You can set a title, due date, and priority.

How do I feed my pet?

Complete tasks to earn coins and experience for your pet. Your pet grows and evolves as you complete more to-dos!

How do I delete a task?

Swipe left on any task in the list and tap Delete.

I lost my data – what do I do?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, data may not be recoverable. We recommend enabling iCloud backup for your device to protect your data.

How do I report a bug?

Please open an issue on our GitHub Issues pagea> or email us at sonnymay@hotmail.coma> with a description of the problem and your device/iOS version.

Privacy Policy
