Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

Tap the plus button on the main screen to create a new to-do item. You can set a title, due date, and priority.

How do I feed my pet?

Complete tasks to earn coins and experience for your pet. Your pet grows and evolves as you complete more to-dos!

How do I delete a task?

Swipe left on any task in the list and tap Delete.

I lost my data - what do I do?

Your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend enabling iCloud backup for your device to protect your data.

How do I report a bug?

Please open an issue on our GitHub Issues page or email us at sonnymay@hotmail.com with a description of the problem and your device and iOS version.

Privacy

ToDoMon does not collect or share any personal data. All your task data is stored locally on your device.
